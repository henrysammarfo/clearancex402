## Step 2 — Pitch visuals (Gamma + B-roll)

*Do this after Step 1 audio is downloaded.*

### 2a. Gamma deck (~15 min)

1. [gamma.app](https://gamma.app) → Create presentation
2. Paste bullets from [GAMMA-PITCH.md](./GAMMA-PITCH.md) → “deep load” section
3. Upload `public/media/pitch-slide-title.png` as slide 1
4. Upload `public/media/architecture.svg` as slide 4
5. Export slides as **PNG** or screen-record scrolling the deck for B-roll

### 2b. App screenshots (~10 min)

Capture from https://clearancex402.vercel.app — see [media/README.md](./media/README.md)

Minimum for pitch: landing, payment-lab probe result, agent-clearance ALLOW.

## Step 3 — Pitch video edit + upload

1. Import 7 audio clips from Step 1
2. Add 1 second gap between clips
3. Lay B-roll from Step 2 over each clip (see timing table in STEP-1)
4. Export 1080p MP4
5. Upload YouTube **Unlisted**
6. Thumbnail: `public/media/pitch-slide-title.png`
